    About Us

    Welcome to Elsamman Illuminations, Where we transform spaces with light! As a lighting company, We pride ourselves on illuminating your world with stunning designs and cutting-edge technology. Whether you're seeking to enhance the ambiance of your home, office, or outdoor spaces, our expert team is dedicated to delivering bespoke lighting solutions that perfectly match your vision. With a vast array of fixtures. Explore our collections. Experience the Radiant Illuminations difference today.

    Spot  

    Spot lighting is the art of precision in illumination. These fixtures are designed to pinpoint and accentuate specific areas or objects, adding depth and drama to any space. Whether highlighting a cherished piece of art, creating a cozy reading nook, or adding a touch of glamour to a room, spot lighting is the secret to achieving the perfect ambiance. Versatile and stylish, spotlights come in various designs, from sleek modern options to classic and timeless choices, ensuring that they seamlessly integrate into any decor. Illuminate with purpose and style using spot lighting to transform your living spaces into captivating showcases of light and shadow.

    Up Lights

    Up lights are the subtle yet powerful magicians of illumination. These fixtures cast a gentle and upward glow, creating an enchanting ambiance that washes walls or enhances architectural features. Ideal for creating soft, inviting atmospheres in both indoor and outdoor settings, up lights add a touch of elegance to any space. With various designs and finishes, they effortlessly elevate your surroundings while preserving a sense of tranquility. Illuminate your space with up lights and let them work their magic, transforming ordinary places into extraordinary environments.

    Track Led Lights

    Track LED lights are the epitome of versatility and efficiency. These fixtures, equipped with adjustable LED bulbs along a track or rail, bring precision to lighting design. Perfect for accentuating artwork, creating task-specific brightness, or setting the mood, track LEDs illuminate spaces with both style and functionality. Their energy-efficient nature, coupled with long-lasting LED technology, ensures you enjoy brilliant illumination while saving on energy costs. Whether in your home, office, or commercial space, track LED lights offer a smart, adaptable lighting solution that brightens any environment with ease.

    Stairs Light

    Stair lights are an essential element of safety and design in any home or commercial space. These fixtures are strategically installed on staircases to provide visibility and reduce the risk of accidents, especially in low-light conditions. Stair lights can range from recessed step lighting to wall-mounted sconces, offering various design options to match the overall aesthetics of the space. Beyond their practical purpose, stair lights also contribute to the ambiance, creating a welcoming and inviting atmosphere while ensuring safe navigation on stairs, both indoors and outdoors. Illuminate your path and elevate your decor with thoughtfully placed stair lights.

    Bollards

    Lighting bollards combine functionality and aesthetics seamlessly. These versatile fixtures serve a dual purpose by providing both illumination and architectural elegance. Placed along pathways, driveways, or outdoor spaces, lighting bollards not only guide the way but also enhance the visual appeal of the environment. Their soft, diffused glow adds a touch of warmth and safety to any setting, while their design complements the surrounding landscape or architecture. Whether used for practicality, ambiance, or a blend of both, lighting bollards are a stylish and functional addition to any outdoor space, harmonizing form and function with grace.

    Led Profile

    LED profiles are the embodiment of modern lighting innovation. These sleek, slim channels house LED strips, offering a clean and elegant way to incorporate lighting into architectural spaces. Whether recessed into walls or ceilings, or surface-mounted for striking visual effects, LED profiles blend seamlessly with their surroundings while providing efficient and stylish illumination. Their versatility allows for creative lighting designs, making them a favorite choice for enhancing aesthetics and functionality in both residential and commercial settings. Illuminate your space with the sophistication and efficiency of LED profiles, where light meets architectural elegance.

    Underwater Light

    Underwater lights, the enchanting stars of aquatic environments, add a captivating dimension to pools, fountains, and water features. These specialized fixtures are designed to thrive in watery settings, creating a mesmerizing play of light beneath the surface. Whether you're looking to illuminate a serene nighttime swim or transform a water feature into a spectacle of color and movement, underwater lights are the key. Their waterproof design, combined with energy-efficient technology, ensures both safety and visual appeal, making them an essential element for any water lover or outdoor enthusiast. Dive into a world of aquatic wonder with underwater lights, where liquid and light unite in a stunning aquatic dance.

    Indoor/Outdoor

    Indoor and outdoor lights are the dynamic duo of lighting design. Indoors, they craft the atmosphere, enhancing comfort and style with a variety of fixtures. Outdoors, they provide safety and curb appeal, guiding the way and elevating your home's exterior charm. Together, they strike a perfect balance, transforming your living spaces into inviting sanctuaries and your outdoor areas into well-lit havens, ensuring both beauty and security around the clock.
